About

I am a senior executive focused on helping organizations navigate complex transformation with clarity, structure, and practical judgment. My work has spanned enterprise systems, operating model change, data governance, reporting, and large-scale business technology initiatives in complex environments.

Over time, my interests have expanded beyond transformation delivery itself toward a broader question: how do powerful technologies, especially AI, change work, institutions, incentives, and public life?

I am especially interested in the future of work, enterprise AI adoption, governance foundations, and the institutional choices leaders must make as technological capability begins to move faster than organizational readiness and social understanding.
